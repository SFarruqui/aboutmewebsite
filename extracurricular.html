The Story Behind Simon's Nonprofit Walivest

Let me introduce you to Walivest, a nonprofit organization with a clear-cut mission. We're all about stepping in before disasters strike, making sure communities are ready for whatever comes their way. But that's not the whole story. We're also here to provide people with the tools and knowledge they need to stand strong and informed. And when the dust settles after a disaster, we're committed to helping communities get back on their feet and rebuild. How did it all begin? Well, after witnessing online the devastation caused by Cyclone Biparjoy in Pakistan and India, I started digging into cyclone research. Conversations with my dad and hearing personal stories brought it home for me. It became clear that it was time to take action and make a positive impact.

Event #1: Ceiling Fan Donation Ceremony at Shihpur Government Primary School

On August 20, 2023, Walivest donated 8 ceiling fans to the Shihpur Government Primary School established in 1920 and located in Sonatola, Bogura, Bangladesh. The headmaster (in the yellow) hosted a Ceiling Fan Donation Ceremony in honor of this donation and I gave a short speech to all the students, staff, and faculty there on the importance of giving back to improve your local community. My father and some of his siblings had attended this school. Furthermore, it was an honor to provide some inspiration for the young children there.

Event #2: Flood Ready Donation Drive at Bhola Sadar Upazila, Bangladesh

On August 22, 2023, Walivest donated 300 life jackets to low-income fishermen of Ilisha, Kachia, Rajapur, and Shibpur unions of Bhola Island, Bangladesh. The event was organized in collaboration with Rajbari Sadar Upazila Samity. There were addresses from me, children who attended the Hazi Nader Mia Bari School, some fishermen's union leaders, and others who helped organize the event. Additionally, this event was featured on Sun News, Ajker Bhola, Bhola 24 News, Daily Bangladesh, World Bangla News, and Bangla TV.

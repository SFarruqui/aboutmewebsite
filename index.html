Personal Life

Read More

I hail from a family with roots in Bangladesh: my parents, Mark and Keya, are first-generation Bengali immigrants. My younger sister, Sonya, recently began her journey as a freshman at Milton Academy. I spent the initial seven years of my life in Dix Hills, New York, before relocating to Frisco, Texas. During my time in Texas, I transitioned between four different schools and moved houses, making the adjustments considerably challenging. After spending another seven years in Texas, I felt drawn to the academic opportunities in New England and decided to apply to boarding schools there. I've now spent three enriching years at Milton, where I've experienced growth not only academically but also on a personal level.

School Activities

Since elementary school, I have always loved the opportunity of being a part of my school community beyond taking classes during the required hours. I desired to have engage in the afterschool opportunities my school offered such as Green Team and Math and Science Team. Although I do not pursue the same afterschool activities, I do love to participate in clubs, publications, and affinities at Milton. From being a co-head of Muslim Students Association to being a founder of Microfinance Club, I strive to learn more about my passions through these organizations.

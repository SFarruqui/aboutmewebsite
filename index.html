Personal Life

This page shows my early years and upbringing.

This page shows my early years and upbringing. I hail from a family with roots in Bangladesh: my parents, Mark and Keya, are first-generation Bengali immigrants. My younger sister, Sonya, recently began her journey as a freshman at Milton Academy. I spent the initial seven years of my life in Dix Hills, New York, before relocating to Frisco, Texas. During my time in Texas, I transitioned between four different schools and moved houses, making the adjustments considerably challenging. After spending another seven years in Texas, I felt drawn to the academic opportunities in New England and decided to apply to boarding schools there. I was fortunate to secure an admission to Milton Academy in March 2021. This achievement prompted yet another move for my family, as we decided to return to New York. I've now spent three enriching years at Milton, where I've experienced growth not only academically but also on a personal level.
